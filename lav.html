Introduction

Located at a distance of about 65 km from Pune, the beautifully planned city of Lavasa is surrounded by the majestic Western Ghats. The city is constructed in the Mulshi Valley and covers a sprawling 25,000 acres. With such mesmerizing views of hills, valleys, and lakes, the city has gained immense popularity among different types of travelers. The beauty of this privately planned city lies in the seamless blend of stunning infrastructure and the enrapturing beauty of nature. Here, you will find different topographical variations such as the lofty Sahyadri range, the beautiful rivers, and the gorgeous valleys.

The tranquil atmosphere combined with the unspoiled beauty of the natural world offers solace from the chaotic strains of everyday life. Lavasa, an awe-inspiring city, boasts a wealth of captivating sites. Uncover the mysterious allure of nature through Tikona Fort, Devkund Waterfall, Dasve Viewpoint, and Tamhini Ghat. For those craving excitement, destinations like Xthrill Adventure Academy and Vortex Splash Pad beckon. Regardless of your travel inclinations, be assured that your yearning for exploration will be met. Lavasa offers a multitude of activities to partake in. Thrill-seekers can engage in exhilarating pursuits like rappelling, hiking, camping, raft building, and biking. However, if your desire is to find tranquility and bask in the beauty of nature, that avenue is open as well. For culinary enthusiasts, Lavasa is a haven. It presents an array of local and global cuisines to satisfy your palate. Perched at an altitude of approximately 3000 feet above sea level, Lavasa experiences a tropical climate, ensuring pleasant weather year-round. Nevertheless, the optimal time to visit falls between the months of September and March.
