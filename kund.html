kundalika valley

(A Mystic Mountain)



Introduction

The river Kundalika, one of the West flowing rivers, in Maharashtra originates in the Sahyadri hills of the Western Ghats near the village Bhamburda. Initially, the river flows in South-West direction up to Patnus village and then turns to North-West till it joins the Arabian sea near village Korlai.
